Chicken Alfredo

Chicken Alfredo is a rich and creamy pasta dish that combines fettuccine noodles with a decadent Alfredo sauce made from butter, cream, and Parmesan cheese. The dish is typically topped with grilled or sautéed chicken, adding protein and flavor. It's a beloved classic in Italian-American cuisine, known for its comforting and indulgent qualities. This dish is quick to prepare, making it perfect for weeknight dinners or special occasions. Serve it with a sprinkle of parsley and extra Parmesan for a delicious finish.

Ingredients are as follows:

  • Fettuccine pasta (about 12 oz)
  • Chicken breast (2 pieces, boneless and skinless)
  • Butter (4 tablespoons)
  • Heavy cream (1 cup)
  • Parmesan cheese (1 cup, grated)
  • Garlic (2 cloves, minced)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Parsley (for garnish, optional)

Steps:

  1. Cook the pasta: Boil a large pot of salted water and cook the fettuccine according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the chicken: Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. In a skillet, heat a tablespoon of butter over medium-high heat. Cook the chicken for about 5–7 minutes per side or until fully cooked. Remove from the skillet and slice.
  3. Make the Alfredo sauce: In the same skillet, melt the remaining butter. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant. Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a gentle simmer.
  4. Combine: Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese and season with salt and pepper. Mix until the cheese has melted and the sauce is smooth. Add the cooked fettuccine to the sauce and toss to combine.
  5. Serve: Plate the pasta and top with sliced chicken. Garnish with parsley and extra Parmesan if desired. Enjoy!
